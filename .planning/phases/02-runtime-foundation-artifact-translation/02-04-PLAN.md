<objective>
Close the two Phase 2 verification gaps WITHOUT touching the already-VERIFIED runtime spine (RUNTIME-01..04) or TRANS-03, and without rewriting the existing 02-01/02-02/02-03 plans.

Gap 1 (BLOCKER, TRANS-01/02): The Bob converters in `runtime-artifact-conversion.cjs` pass the body through unneutralized — `.claude`/`~/.claude`/`$HOME/.claude`/`./.claude` paths and the deprecated `gsd:` colon form survive verbatim into emitted `.bob/` artifacts, pointing Bob at paths that do not exist under a `.bob` install. Every peer converter (Antigravity, Cline) neutralizes both; Bob is the only one that does not. This plan adds a `convertClaudeToBobContent` pass mirroring `convertClaudeToAntigravityContent` and applies it to both converters, and updates the golden fixtures so the diff fails until the fix lands.

Gap 2 (WARNING, TRANS-04/05): Three silent-failure edges in `src/bob-adapter.cjs` contradict the loud-failure intent these requirements are sold on — a non-mapping YAML root silently drops the gsd mode (or throws opaquely), a null candidate is silently admitted as supported, and a nameless candidate emits a malformed `undefined:` roster line. This plan guards all three to fail loud / exclude with a concrete reason, with regression tests.

Purpose: deliver the "provably correct against Bob's documented contract" / parity-first guarantee the phase goal asserts, and the loud-failure guarantee TRANS-04/05 claim.
Output: `convertClaudeToBobContent` (new), updated Bob converters, updated golden fixtures + golden tests, hardened `mergeCustomModes`/`gateArtifact`/`buildSupportRoster`, new regression tests.
